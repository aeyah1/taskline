Your data, your device
Everything you need

Built around how you actually work.

Six features that each earn their place. No bloat, no half-finished ideas, no learning curve.

Sign in your way

Register once, then log in using either your username or your email. A strength meter helps you pick a real password.

A dashboard that talks back

Color-coded stat cards, today's focus, the week ahead, and a 7-day completion sparkline so you can see momentum.

Full task CRUD

Create, edit, complete, and delete tasks with priority, due date, and a confirm dialog on destructive actions.

Search, filter, sort

Find any task by keyword. Filter by status or priority. Sort by smart, newest, oldest, due date, or priority.

Real feedback

Toasts confirm every action. Inline errors appear as you submit. The strength meter and overdue chips speak up.

You own your data

Export everything as JSON whenever you want. Re-import on a new device. Clear it all from the danger zone. GDPR-style portability, no fuss.

How it works

From zero to focused in three steps.

Open it, sign in, start working. That's the whole onboarding.

Create your account

Pick a username, drop in your email, choose a real password. The meter tells you when it's strong.

Capture what's next

Press N or use the quick-add on the dashboard. Set a due date and a priority if it matters.

Finish the day strong

Check tasks off as you go. Watch your completion rate climb. Export your data whenever you want.

Built with care

Plain web. Zero dependencies.

Just HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks to learn, no node_modules to break, no build step. Open the folder, double-click index.html, and it works.

Separated concerns

Persistence, auth, validation, UI, and business logic each live in their own file. Single-responsibility from the start.

Design tokens

A single tokens file drives every color, spacing, radius, and motion value. Change one variable, the whole app updates.

Accessibility-aware

Real focus rings, ARIA labels on icon buttons, semantic HTML, and a reduced-motion preference fallback.
